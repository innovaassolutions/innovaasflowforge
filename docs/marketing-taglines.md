## Alternative Options

### Consultant-focused
- "Assess smarter. Advise better."
- "Your consulting practice, amplified."
- "Scale your expertise, not your hours."

### Process-focused
- "From interviews to insights, intelligently."
- "Structured discovery. Synthesized clarity."
- "Every stakeholder heard. Every insight captured."

### Outcome-focused
- "AI-facilitated assessments. Expert-level insights."
- "Transform how you assess. Elevate how you advise."
- "Where deep discovery meets decisive action."

### Concise/Punchy
- "Discover. Synthesize. Advise."
- "Consulting intelligence, accelerated."

---

## Usage Notes

- The recommended tagline works well for hero sections, email signatures, and brief marketing contexts
- "AI-facilitated discovery. Expert-level insights." is a strong alternative when emphasizing the technology differentiator
- Avoid leading with "AI" in contexts where it may feel cold or generic
