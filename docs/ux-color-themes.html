Theme Comparison

Key characteristics of each variation

A. Warm Cream
  • Warmest base tone (#FAF7F2)
  • Burnt orange accent (#E85A1B)
  • Most inviting, artisanal feel
  • Best for: Boutique consulting firms
  • Potential concern: May feel too casual
B. Off-White Terracotta
  • Neutral cream base (#FDFCFA)
  • Terracotta accent (#C4562A)
  • Most grounded, mature feel
  • Best for: Traditional enterprises
  • Potential concern: Accent may feel muted
C. Pearl Vibrant (Recommended)
  • Cleanest near-white base (#FFFEFB)
  • Vibrant orange accent (#F25C05)
  • Best balance of premium and energy
  • Best for: Modern transformation consulting
  • Aligns with FlowForge brand positioning

Theme Personalities

The emotional character each theme conveys

A
The Artisan
Handcrafted quality and personal attention. Like visiting a specialty boutique where every detail is considered. Warm and approachable, yet refined.
B
The Sage
Wisdom and experience. Like sitting in a well-appointed study with leather-bound books. Grounded, trustworthy, and timeless in its appeal.
C
The Innovator
Forward-thinking clarity. Like the clean lines of a modern gallery where ideas shine. Professional yet energetic, premium yet accessible.

Recommendation: Theme C - Pearl Vibrant

This theme best captures FlowForge's positioning as a modern AI-powered consulting platform. The clean base allows content to breathe while the vibrant orange accent provides energy and draws attention to key actions. It feels premium without being stuffy, innovative without being cold.
