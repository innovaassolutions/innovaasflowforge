Key Findings

Enrollment process rated highly with 85% of parents describing it as "smooth" or "very smooth"

Based on 18 parent interviews

Phone-based issue resolution receives strong marks, with front office staff consistently praised for helpfulness

Based on 24 stakeholder interviews

Email response times average 4.2 days, significantly below parent expectations of 24-48 hours

Based on 15 parent interviews mentioning email delays

Event information distributed across multiple channels creates confusion; 62% of parents report missing at least one school event

Based on 18 parent interviews

"When I can get someone on the phone, they're always helpful and really seem to care. It's just frustrating when emails go unanswered for days. I end up calling anyway, which makes me wonder why we have email at all."

- Parent, Grade 10 student

Priority Recommendations

  • 1

    Implement an email auto-acknowledgment system that confirms receipt and sets expectations for response timing (target: 24-48 hours)

    High Impact - Quick Win
  • 2

    Consolidate event communications into a single, unified school calendar accessible via the parent portal with push notifications

    High Impact - Medium Effort
  • 3

    Establish clear email ownership and response SLAs for different inquiry types (academic, administrative, scheduling)

    Medium Impact - Quick Win
