## Situational Intelligence Modules (SIMs)

Time-bound, event-triggered, exposure-sensitive.

SIMs explain **how something specific landed**, using the same stakeholder logic.

### SIM #1: Leadership Transition & Stability (High Impact, High Risk)

Leadership transitions are one of the highest predictors of:
- parent withdrawals  
- staff exits  
- trust erosion  

Damage often appears 1–2 terms later.

This module surfaces early confidence erosion before exits occur.

**Stakeholders:**
- Parents  
- Teachers  
- Non-Teaching Staff  
- Leadership / SLT  
- Students (12+, optional)

Measures:
- continuity vs reset  
- clarity vs confusion  
- confidence vs anxiety  

---

### SIM #2: Open Day & Admissions Experience (High Exposure)

Open days are performative and high-stakes, yet rarely assessed structurally.

This module answers:
> Did what we intended actually land the way we think it did?

Stakeholders:
- Prospective Parents  
- Admissions / Admin Staff  
- Leadership  

---

### SIM #3: Staff Onboarding & Retention Health

High turnover is caused by unseen accumulation.

Stakeholders:
- New teachers (0–6 months)  
- Existing teachers  
- Non-teaching staff  
- Leadership  

---

### SIM #4: Change Impact Intelligence

Measures the gap between:
- leadership intent  
- lived experience  

Tracks:
- clarity vs confusion  
- trust vs anxiety  
- adoption vs resistance
