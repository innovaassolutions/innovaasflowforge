## Target School Categories

### Category A: Leadership Transition Schools

**Why:** Highest pain, highest value, fastest conversion

| Indicator | What to Look For |
|-----------|------------------|
| New Head | Within last 18 months |
| SLT changes | Multiple new hires |
| Board transition | New chair or significant turnover |
| Public signals | Press releases, website changes |

**Approach:** Position as "grounding" during uncertainty

### Category B: Pre-Inspection Schools

**Why:** Need assurance data, budget available, urgency

| Indicator | What to Look For |
|-----------|------------------|
| CIS/WASC cycle | 6-18 months before visit |
| Self-study phase | Active preparation |
| Leadership anxiety | Seeking external validation |

**Approach:** Position as "preparation intelligence"

### Category C: Enrollment Pressure Schools

**Why:** Need to understand parent confidence drivers

| Indicator | What to Look For |
|-----------|------------------|
| Waitlist shrinkage | Declining demand signals |
| Marketing increase | Defensive positioning |
| Competitor growth | Market share pressure |
| Fee sensitivity | Discussions about pricing |

**Approach:** Position as "confidence intelligence"

### Category D: Staff Retention Challenges

**Why:** Need to understand teacher experience

| Indicator | What to Look For |
|-----------|------------------|
| High turnover | Above 15% annually |
| Recruitment difficulty | Extended vacancies |
| Glassdoor signals | Negative reviews |
| Exit interview patterns | Common themes |

**Approach:** Position as "retention intelligence"

## Outreach Strategy

### Warm Approach (Preferred)

```
1. Identify connection to Head or Board member
2. Request informal introduction
3. Coffee/call: share philosophy, not pitch
4. If resonance: offer to share pilot framework
5. If interest: formal pilot proposal
```

### Conference Approach

```
1. Attend EARCOS, FOBISIA, CIS conferences
2. Participate in leadership conversations
3. Share perspective (not product)
4. Follow up with interested leaders
5. Relationship-first, pilot-second
```

### Content Approach

```
1. Publish thought leadership on institutional intelligence
2. Share anonymised insights (with permission)
3. Build reputation as trusted advisor
4. Inbound interest from aligned leaders
```

## Qualification Criteria

### Must-Have

- [ ] Premium school ($25k+ fees)
- [ ] Leadership willing to hear uncomfortable truths
- [ ] Budget authority to approve pilot
- [ ] Timing alignment (not mid-crisis)

### Nice-to-Have

- [ ] Warm introduction available
- [ ] Recent trigger event (transition, inspection, etc.)
- [ ] Reference potential (case study willing)
- [ ] Multi-school group (expansion opportunity)

### Disqualifiers

- [ ] Looking for "free trial"
- [ ] Want dashboard, not intelligence
- [ ] Leadership defensive posture
- [ ] Crisis mode (too hot)
- [ ] Board conflict (too political)
