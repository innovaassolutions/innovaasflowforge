Brand Concept: Unlocking Flow

FlowForge helps organizations unlock their hidden potential. By working through an organization's current state and identifying opportunities for improvement, we unlock Flow - new value, new revenue opportunities, cultural alignment, and enhanced organizational value for everyone. The logo represents this moment of breakthrough and release - two shapes opening to reveal flowing energy.

Design Notes

  • Left Shape (Orange): Represents the organization's current state, the constraint that's being opened
  • Right Shape (Teal): Represents the desired future state, the potential waiting to be unlocked
  • Central Flow (Gradient): The flowing energy released when constraints are removed - the value, revenue, and alignment that emerges
  • Visual Metaphor: Two barriers opening outward, revealing dynamic flow between them - the moment of breakthrough
  • Scalability: Clean geometry ensures the icon remains recognizable at 16px while being visually striking at larger sizes
